About

With FitJacket, fitness is more easy and accessible than ever before. Users can track their progress and get personalized workout recommendations. FitJacket offers AI-powered workout plans that adapt to users goals and progress. FitJacket is web-based, so users can access their fitness plan from any device with internet.

To make fitness fun, FitJacket helps cultivate a fitness community. With FitJacket, users can connect with friends, join challenges, and celebrate their achievements. FitJacket includes challenges, leaderboards, and achievement badges to help motivate users. FitJacket also integrates fitness tracking APIs to get real-time data from external devices and apps, improving the accuracy of fitness insights.
